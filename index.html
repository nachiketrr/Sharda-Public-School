Welcome to official site of Sharda Public School, Ajmer Saundane, Tal. Satana, Dist. Nashik

Sharda Public School provides a friendly and secular environment with the aim of building confident, respectful and responsible individuals. The school nurtures and develops each individual’s capabilities so as to integrate a harmonious development of the child’s intellectual, emotional and physical capacities. Self confidence and the right set of values form the cornerstone of a successful and happy life, and we at Sharda Public School guide our students to grow up with a sense of social awareness, civic responsibility and personal growth.
Many opportunities are provided to support students as they develop into productive and informed participants in a global community.

Our sincere, dedicated and hard working team of —– staff members are devoted to the overall development of more than —- students on roll and their consolidated efforts have resulted into making the school one of the premier educational institutions in the Education System of India. We strive to inculcate in our students an awareness and belief in all moral values, respect for the dignity of work, and to prepare them for securing their place in society as noble and respected citizens.

Playing Games

Children playing outside/inside get aerobic exercise and gain skills, such as pushing and pulling play equipment. Studies show children burn more calories outdoors, helping to prevent obesity and strengthen bones and muscles. It helps to increase concentration on studies.

Annual Gathering

The annual functions are very important in a school because it will increase the talent and all extracurricular activities of a child. They can think and realize the talents inside of them. Nowadays kids are very interested in different activities like dancing, singing, painting, playing instruments etc.

Festival Celebration

Festivals enable children as well as elders to celebrate the joy of being together and their happiness with family and friends. This further promotes the development of our cultural heritage and stimulates the feelings of oneness, cooperation, and preservation of our old traditions and customs.
